Visa Interview Dress Code

Professional attire guidelines for your visa appointment

For Men

  • Shirt: Light-colored formal shirt (white, light blue, or pastel)
  • Trousers: Dark-colored formal pants (black, navy, or grey)
  • Belt & Shoes: Black/brown leather belt and matching polished shoes
  • Optional: A simple tie (plain or subtle stripes) if you want a sharper look
  • Grooming: Well-trimmed beard or clean-shaven, neatly combed hair

For Women

  • Indian formal: Plain cotton/silk saree in soft colors or salwar kameez (not flashy, avoid too much embroidery)
  • Footwear: Closed-toe shoes or neat sandals (avoid high heels or flip-flops)
  • Jewelry/Makeup: Minimal â€” small earrings, light chain, light makeup, no heavy accessories
  • Hair: Neatly tied back or styled simply

What to Avoid

  • Jeans + T-shirt (too casual)
  • Over-accessorizing (heavy chains, flashy watches)
  • Strong perfumes (visa halls are small, and officers may be sensitive)
  • Unkempt look (wrinkled clothes, untidy hair)

Pro Tip

Dress as if you are going for a job interview or an official business meeting.

Present yourself professionally to make a positive impression
