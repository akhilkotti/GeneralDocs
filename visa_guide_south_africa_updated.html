South Africa Visa Application Guide

  1. Identify visa type
    Choose the right visa for your trip.
    The first step is to determine which visa type you need, and check whether you’re eligible to apply for it.
    Click to learn more: Visa Categories & Requirements
    If your documents are not in English, you may need translations during application.
  2. Begin your application
    Download and complete the visa application form.
    Bring printed form with required documents to the Visa Application Centre.
    Application form download link
  3. Book an appointment
    You may drop off your form without appointment — check opening hours:
    Locate Application Centre
  4. Pay your fees
    Pay visa fees during submission at the centre (Cash / QR / Card).
    Check fee details:
    Visa Fees Information
  5. Visit the Visa Application Centre
    Submit your completed form in person.
    What happens at the centre?
    See Process Details
  6. Track your application
    Track via Reference Number and Last Name:
    Track Online
    Alternative Tracking
  7. Collect your passport
    Collect at the centre or request courier return.
    Representatives must carry authorization + ID.

Important: SMS updates unavailable if DND is active on mobile.
